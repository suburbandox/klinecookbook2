Fran's Tex-Mex Dip<br><br>
3 medium ripe avocados<br> 2 T lemon juice<br> 1/2 t salt<br> 1/4 t pepper<br> 1 c sour cream<br> 1/2 c mayonnaise<br> 1 pkg. taco seasoning mix<br> 2 10.5  oz. cans plain or jalapeno bean dip or refried beans<br> 1 c green onions with tops, chopped<br> 3 medium tomatoes, chopped<br> 2 cans pitted ripe olives, chopped and drained<br> 1 8 oz. pkg. sharp cheddar cheese, shredded<br> tortilla chips<br><br>

1. Peel, pit, and mash avocados in a bowl with lemon juice, salt and pepper. Combine sour cream, mayo, and taco seasonings. Mix well. <br>
2. Spread bean dip in a large shallow serving bowl; top with avocado mixture; layer with sour cream and taco mixture. <br>
3. Sprinkle with chopped onions, tomatoes, and olives. Cover with shredded cheese. Serve on a platter of tortilla chips.
