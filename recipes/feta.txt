Feta Appetizers<br><br>
Crumble feta, beaten egg, lots of black pepper together until mixture is pretty gooey.<br><br>

Cut frozen defrosted filo dough into 2" by 8" strips. Take individual strip, paint with melted butter and put two other strips on top, painting in between with melted butter. Put about a t of feta mixture in top corner. Fold strip all the way down in a triangle form. Paint top of appetizer with more melted butter or margarine. Bake on a greased strip at 425 for 15-20 minutes.
