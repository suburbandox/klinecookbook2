Tomato Chutney (Serves 7-8)<br><br>
Combine: <br>2 cups chopped fresh or canned tomatoes<br> 1 medium chopped onion<br> 3 tablespoons lemon juice<br> 2 tablespoons vinegar<br> 1 tablespoon sugar<br> pinch of salt and pepper
<br><br>Garnish with fresh coriander if available.
<br><br>Serve as side dish with rice and curry.
