Cabbage Soup<br><br>
1. Saute 2-3 chopped onions and garlic and fresh hot pepper in olive oil until translucent (medium heat), add 1/2 to 3/4 of small chopped cabbage and more oil, cook 10-15 minutes.<br>
2. Add 1 large can of whole canned tomatoes (break them up with fingers). <br>
3. Add 6-8 cups of broth (vegetarian, chicken, or beef), 1 15 oz. can of tomato sauce, 1 tablespoon of sugar, 1-2 tablespoons of vinegar. Simmer 30 minutes.<br>
4. Add tabasco sauce, salt, pepper, red wine, all to taste. Cook down a little further.<br>
5. Note, you can add chunks of beef or Italian sausage at beginning when frying onions and garlic. If vegetarian, you may have to add additional spices.
