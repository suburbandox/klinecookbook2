Berenjena con Queso (Eggplant with Cheese from Puerto Rico)<br><br>
2 lbs. eggplant<br> 6 c water<br> 1 1/2 T salt<br> 1/4 lb. (1 c) grated parmesan<br> 1 egg<br> 1/2 c butter<br> 2 T butter<br> 3 T cracker crumbs.<br><br>

1. Wash eggplant and drop, without peeling, into the water with salt. Bring to a boil, cover pot, and cook rapidly for 30 minutes. Remove eggplant from pot, peel and mash with a fork (a potato masher also works). <br>
2. Add grated cheese, egg and 1/2 c butter. Mix well and put mixture into greased glass baking dish (1 quart).<br>
3. Sprinkle with cracker crumbs and dot with remaining butter. Bake in preheated oven at 375 for 30 minutes. It reheats well and can be prepared ahead of time and baked just prior to serving. Makes 4 servings.
