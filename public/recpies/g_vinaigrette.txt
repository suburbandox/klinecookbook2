Geezer's Vinaigrette Dressing<br><br>
Fresh mushed garlic<br> salt<br> Dijon mustard<br> dry mustard<br> fresh or dry spices (chives, basil, oregano, tarragon)<br> a little soy sauce<br> lemon juice<br> oil (combination of vegetable and olive if olive is particularly strong) and vinegar (half balsamic, half wine) in a 2 parts oil to 1 vinegar combination. <br>Proportions are to taste.<br><br>
Besides serving over salad, this is great to marinate mushrooms and celery or over cold steamed green beans.
