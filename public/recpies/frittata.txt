Artichoke Frittata - Kay McGutticken<br><br>
1/2 lb. sliced mushrooms<br> 1 c chopped onions (little less)<br> 1 T vegetable oil/butter<br> 1 ten oz. pack frozen spinach<br> 5 lg. eggs<br> 2-6 oz. marinated artichokes - drained well<br> 1 1/2 c sharp cheddar cheese<br><br> 

Saute mushrooms and onions in oil. Thaw spinach and drain well. Beat eggs lightly and combine with mushrooms, artichokes, spinach, and cheese. Bake in a covered casserole for 45 minutes at 350.
