Quiche Lorraine<br><br>
Dough: <br>1 cup flour<br> 1/4 t baking powder<br> 1 stick cold butter<br> 1 T sugar<br> 1 t water if necessary. <br><br>
Sift dry ingredients. Add butter. Mix, adding water if necessary. Put in 10 inch pie plate. Bake in preheated oven at 375 degrees for 15 minutes.
<br><br>
Filling: <br>1 small onion fried in bacon fat until transparent<br> 1 cup grated cheese<br> 3 eggs<br> 1.5 cups milk or light cream<br> 1/8 t salt<br> pepper<br> nutmeg (optional)<br><br>
Spread bacon, onion, and cheese on bottom of dough. Blend eggs, milk, and salt and pepper. Pour into shell. Bake 15 minutes at 450 degrees.
