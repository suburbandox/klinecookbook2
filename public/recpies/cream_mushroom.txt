Cream of Mushroom Soup - six servings<br><br>
6 T margarine<br> 3 cups chicken bouillon (made with 3 cubes)<br> 1 medium onion, thinly sliced<br> 1/2 lb. fresh mushrooms, sliced<br> 1 bay leaf<br> 3 T flour<br> 1/8 t pepper<br> 3/4 cup evaporated milk (I would bet you can use cream or milk here). 
<br><br>
1. Melt margarine in heavy pan. Add onion and saute a few minutes - do not brown. Add mushrooms and cook another 3-4 minutes, stirring.<br>
2. Remove from heat and blend in flour. Add bouillon and bay leaf and pepper.<br>
3. Bring to boil, stirring constantly.<br>
4. Simmer for 5 minutes. Add evaporated milk - do not boil after this.<br>
5. Remove bay leaf before serving.
