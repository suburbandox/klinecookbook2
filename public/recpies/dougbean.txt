Doug's Bean Soup<br>
1. Cook in a Crock pot at 350 degrees for four hours, the following: 1.5 cup red beans, 1.5 cup yellow beans; 4 hot peppers; 2 tomatoes; 4 stalks celery; 1 can tomato paste; 2 red potatoes; 2 red onions; 2.5 cups water; 1 cup red wine; 4 mushrooms.<br>
2. Essentially you are filling pot until full with assorted vegetables and then cook.<br>
3. Add assortment of spices and pepper to taste.
