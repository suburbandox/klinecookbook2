Ilona's Crab/Shrimp Dip<br><br>
Mix in food processor: <br>11 oz. cream cheese<br> 2 T Worcestershire sauce<br> 2 T garlic powder<br> Add 1 medium onion, finely chopped.
<br><br>
Spread mixture on large platter. Pour on 3/4 bottle of Heinz chili sauce. Sprinkle 3/4 lb. of backfin crab or shrimp on top. Cover with lots of chopped parsley.
