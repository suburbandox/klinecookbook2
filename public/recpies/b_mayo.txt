Blender Mayonnaise - Life<br><br>
2 eggs; 2 T lemon juice; 1 T vinegar; 1/2 t dry mustard; 1 t salt; 1 1/2 cups vegetable oil.<br><br>
Combine eggs, lemon juice, vinegar and seasoning in blender; cover and blend at high speed for 30 seconds until well mixed. With blender running at medium speed; take off cover and slowly drip in oil (very slowly). Blend until thick and smooth.
