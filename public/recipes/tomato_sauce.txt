Tomato Sauce<br><br>
Saute onions and garlic until tender. Add chopped fresh or canned tomatoes with fresh or dry herbs (oregano and basil). Add canned tomato sauce, dried chili peppers, a little salt and sugar. Can add dry red wine. Simmer for one hour until thick.<br><br> 
For use over spaghetti, good just with parmesan.<br><br>
Can also saute green peppers, mushrooms, and sweet or hot Italian sausage with onion and garlic.
