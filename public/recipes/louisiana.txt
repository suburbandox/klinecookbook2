Creamy Louisiana Marinade<br><br>
In a medium-size bowl whisk:<br> 1/2 cup mayonnaise<br> 1/2 teaspoon creole mustard<br> 2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice<br> 2 green onions or green and red onion<br> 1/2 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce<br> 1 teaspoon rosemary, basil, and oregano (fresh) or 1/4 teaspoon dried of each<br> 1/2 teaspoon paprika<br> 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground pepper<br> 1/4 teaspoon cayenne<br> 1/4 teaspoon salt<br><br> Taste and adjust seasonings. Make marinade one day ahead to blend flavors.
Coat food with marinade, cover and refrigerate for 3 hours or overnight. <br><br>Great with salmon.
