Sheila's Mushroom Appetizers<br><br>
May be made 2 days ahead, but not frozen.<br>
8 ounce package of cream cheese<br> 1 T minced onion.<br><br>
1. Slice 1/4 lb. mushrooms into tiny pieces. Fry in butter.<br>
2. Cream cheese to soften. Add mushroom mixture. Add salt and pepper. Toast rounds of bread. On untoasted side of bread put on mushroom spread. Broil until done.
