Almond Pate<br><br>
1 cup minced onion; 2 T freshly minced parsley; 1 T butter; 1/2 t freshly ground cumin; 1 t tamari; 1 small clove garlic, minced; 1/2 t savory; salt and freshly ground pepper; 1/2 t fine herbs; 2-4 T mayonnaise or sour cream; 1 c raw almonds, ground fairly fine; lemon slices; 1 1/2 cups plus 2 T bread crumbs toasted in the oven.
<br><br>
Yields 1 cup.
<br><br>
1. Cook onions in butter over low heat with cumin, savor and fine herbs until soft.<br>
2. Scrape onion mixture into bowl. Using fingers work in almonds, bread crumbs, parsley, tamari and garlic.<br>
3. Taste and season with salt and pepper.<br>
4. Gradually mix in the mayonnaise or sour cream until mixture holds together.<br>
5. Shape into log or press into serving dish and garnish with overlapping slices of lemon.
