Hollandaise Sauce (3/4 cup)<br><br>
1 1/2 T hot lemon juice<br> 3 egg yolks<br> 1/4 lb. melted butter<br> dash white or cayenne pepper<br> 2 T boiling water<br> 1/2 T salt<br><br>
Beat egg yolks in blender until yellow and thick. Add salt and pepper and whirl. Add butter and lemon juice, a little at a time while the blender is at a medium speed. Drizzle in boiling water. Makes about 3/4 cup. Serve over vegetables. If it hardens set bowl in warm water.
