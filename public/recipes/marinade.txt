Special Marinade for Formal Entertaining<br><br>
(Good for broiling London broil)<br><br>
1 1/2 c salad oil<br> 2 T coarse pepper<br> 3/4 cup soy sauce<br> 1/2 cup wine vinegar<br> 1/4 c Worcestershire sauce<br> 2 cloves garlic<br> 2 T dry mustard<br> 1/3 cup lemon juice.<br><br>
Combine all ingredients. Stir to blend. Make sauce the day before. Makes 3 cups.
