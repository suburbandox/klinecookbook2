Fran's Mexican Dip<br><br>
2 cans refried beans<br> 16 oz. sour cream<br> 1 pkg. taco seasoning mix<br> cheddar cheese <br>(chopped tomatoes, onions, olives optional)
<br><br>
1. Mix beans and taco seasoning. Place in casserole; top with sour cream.<br>
2. Sprinkle cheddar cheese and optional items on top. <br>
3. Bake 25 minutes at 350. Serve with nacho chips.
