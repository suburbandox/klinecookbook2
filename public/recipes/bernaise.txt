Bernaise Sauce<br><br>
2 T wine (any<br> 2 T lemon juice<br> 1 T chopped onion<br> 1 T tarragon<br> 3 egg yolks<br> 1 stick melted butter<br> 1 T cider vinegar<br> 1/4 t salt<br> 1/4 t pepper.<br><br>
Combine wine, vinegar, tarragon, onion and pepper in pan. Bring to boil and cook rapidly until almost all liquid disappears. In pan, heat butter until bubbling. Place the rest of the ingredients in blender. Turn high, and slowly add herb mixture and butter. Makes 1 cup. Good with beef fondue.
