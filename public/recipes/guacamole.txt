Tonie'ss Guacamole Dip (2 cups)<br><br>
1 medium ripe avocado<br> 2 T lemon juice<br> 1 4 oz. can diced pimentos, drained<br> 1 small chopped cucumber<br> 1 medium chopped green pepper<br> 1 crushed garlic clove<br> 1/2 tsp. salt<br> dash of Tabasco. <br><br>
In bowl mash together avocado and lemon juice until blended. Stir in remaining ingredients. Chill.
