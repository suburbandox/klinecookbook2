Cream of Spinach Soup<br><br>
1. Cover with water and steam until tender and puree in own water: 1 carrot. 1 onion, 1 clove garlic, 1 potato.<br>
2. Steam 1 pound spinach in 1 cup water until wilted. Puree.<br>
3. Whisk into roux: 1/3 cup flour into 1/3 cup melted butter. Whisk in 2 cups milk. Cook over very low heat, stirring until thickened.<br>
4. Add spinach to roux along with 1/2 teaspoon salt, pepper, 1/2 teaspoon basil, pinch nutmeg, pinch thyme, parsley and marjoram.<br>
5. Add pureed vegetables to roux and spinach.<br>
6. Adjust seasoning.<br>
7. If too thick add milk.<br>
8. Heat at very low flame and stir until creamy, green and fragrant.
