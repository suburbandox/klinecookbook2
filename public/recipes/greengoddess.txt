Green Goddess Dressing - Jody<br><br>
An easy blender salad fixing<br><br>
1/4 c chopped parsley (no stems)<br> 1 T tarragon vinegar<br> 1 T lemon juice<br> 1 c sour cream<br> 1/2 c mayonnaise (homemade)<br> 1/3 c chopped onion or scallion<br> 1 or 2 cloves garlic<br> salt and pepper to taste. <br><br>Blend and enjoy.
