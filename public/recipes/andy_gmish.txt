Andy's G'Mish<br><br>
2 cans black beans, 1 can refried beans; jar hot salsa; chopped onion, garlic (either powder or finely minced); few tomatoes – chopped up; pepper; lots of jalapenos
<br><br>
1. Mix all together and put in pan. Bake 350 for 40 minutes, until bubbly. Grate cheddar cheese, put on top of mixture. Bake 10 more minutes until cheese is grated.
<br>2. Serve over a tortilla or with nacho chips.
