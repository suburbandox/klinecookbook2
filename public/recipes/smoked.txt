Smoked Salmon Spread (Staton Hills Winery)<br><br>
1 lb. smoked salmon<br> 1 medium onion finely chopped<br> 1 T chopped capers<br> 1/2 c mayonnaise<br> 1/2 c sour cream<br> freshly ground pepper<br> 3/4 c chopped celery<br> 2 T chopped parsley <br><br>

Line a round bowl with Saran wrap. Mix all but parsley and put in bowl for 2 hours. Invert and garnish with parsley. Slice French bread (baguette). Butter and brown on both sides. Serve spread on toast.
