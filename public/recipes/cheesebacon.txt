Cheese-bacon puffs<br><br>
1 egg<br> 1/2 cup finely grated cheddar cheese<br> 1 tsp. grated onions<br> 1/4 tsp. dry mustard<br> 24 one inch bacon squares<br> 3 slices bread.<br><br>
Preheat oven to 375. Beat egg with fork, add all ingredients except bacon. Stir until well-blended. Cut bread into 12 squares. Arrange on pan. Spoon heaping spoonfuls of cheese mixture onto each. Top with bacon. Bake 15 minutes until bread is toasted and topping puffy.
