Mayonnaise (1.3 cups)
2 Tbs. cider vinegar, 1 egg, 3/4 tsp. salt, 1/2 tsp dry mustard, dash white pepper, 1 cup oil.
In blender place all ingredients but oil. Blend at high speed for 5 seconds. Add 1/4 cup oil and blend at low speed, 5 seconds. Add 1/4 cup oil and blend at low speed, 5 seconds. Turn blender to high and add rest of oil steadily. If mixture separates, beat in 1 Tbs. hot water.
